🎙️ Conan O'Brien Needs a Fan · 2021–2026

Every Fan, Mapped.

For years, Conan O'Brien interviewed the biggest names in Hollywood on Conan O'Brien Needs a Friend and realized he'd completely lost touch with regular folks. So in 2021, he course-corrected. Conan O'Brien Needs a Fan is a weekly segment that invites civilians to chat with Conan and the Chill Chums. No fame or fancy mic required. Just Conan, desperately hoping they'll become celebrities someday. Hundreds of fans were invited. Some left bigger fans. Some left… very confused. This map tracks every fan who answered the call anyway.
